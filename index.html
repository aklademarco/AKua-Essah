About Me

Essah Akua is a dynamic young leader and mental health advocate committed to creating lasting impact through education, innovation, and mentorship.

At just 20 years old, she is a final-year Computer Science student at Ghana Communication Technology University, driven by an unrelenting passion for technology and its power to transform lives.

She believes that technology should be accessible to all, regardless of background or academic standing. Determined to break barriers in tech education, she is focused on ensuring that children across Africa get hands-on exposure to innovation.

Essah is equally fascinated by the complexity of the human mind- how people think, behave, and perceive the world. This curiosity has led her to personal mental health research, where she is passionate about bridging the gap in understanding for individuals living with mental illnesses.

Accolades

Essah’s dedication to technology, education, and advocacy has earned her several recognitions, including:

  • ✨ Techpreneur of the Year 2025
  • ✨ Vodafone Challenge Award Winner
  • ✨ Top 10 in Huawei Seeds of the Future Initiative Exams
  • ✨ Miss Eloquent 2022
  • ✨ Executive Ambassador for Developers in Vogue

Her leadership journey began early, earning the ranks of Corporal in Junior High School Cadet and Sergeant in Senior High School Cadet Corps, where she developed discipline, resilience, and a strong sense of responsibility. These qualities continue to define her as she takes on greater leadership roles in her field.

As a mentor, Essah is passionate about guiding young individuals who aspire to enter the world of technology and leadership. Her mentorship style focuses on empowerment and skill development, ensuring that those she guides gain the confidence and tools to succeed.

Looking ahead, her vision is to create a future where every child in Africa has access to technology and the opportunity to innovate, regardless of their circumstances. She is actively working toward this goal by collaborating with organizations, institutions, and changemakers who share her mission.

Essah Akua is open to public speaking engagements, collaborations, and mentorship opportunities. If you're looking to connect with a visionary leader driving real impact in technology and mental health, she is ready to make a difference
